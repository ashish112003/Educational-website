Frequently Asked Questions

Will I be able to get a demo class before taking the course?

Certainly,You will be getting 1 demo class for free to pick your tutor and incase you need a change in tutor, you will always be able to do it.

How does the whole process go by?

Once you request for a tutor with the asked details, we do a best-match analysis to find you the right tutor.A trial class slot will be allocated.After the trial, based on your review and interest it's upto you whether you go ahead with the teacher or not. Post your consent, we finalize the time slots as per your and tutor's convenience. Post your payment, we create a Whatsapp group with Tutor, student and parents where we manage all the communications/ queries/ concerns regarding the classes, so you will always be in loop.

How does the trial class work? Can I take multiple?

Yeah, You are allowed to take multiple trial classes incase you are not satisfied with the trial class of the tutor we allotted.

What is the payment process after the trial class works?

Once the teacher is allotted, the payment will discussed mutually by the Institute and the parent, The negotiable price will be considered.

What if my tutor has to go on a sudden leave?

We don't deny that this will never happen. In that case, you need not pay the price of the classes which are not taken.

How do I change my Tutor if Iam not satisfied?

Certainly,You will be able to change the tutor if you are not satisfied with the allocated tutor. You will once again get a trail class by the new teacher and if that works, you can go ahead or else the loop runs till the new teacher gets allocated.

Certainly,You will be getting 1 demo class for free to pick your tutor and incase you need a change in tutor, you will always be able to do it.

Will I be charged for the trial class?

No, You will not be charged for the trial class.
